🔐 Privacy Policy

Focus N Grow | Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, phone number, school name, and grade level when you register. We also collect study session data, mood logs, and app usage information to provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide AI coaching, track your study progress, and improve the app. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase PostgreSQL servers located in the EU. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information.

4. Children's Privacy

Focus N Grow is designed for students aged 13 and above. For users under 18, we encourage parental supervision and provide parent monitoring features.

5. School Data

When students join a school's institutional mode, study activity and mood data may be shared with that school's teachers and administrators for educational purposes.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings → Delete Account. You can also request data export by contacting us.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: focusngrow@gmail.com
